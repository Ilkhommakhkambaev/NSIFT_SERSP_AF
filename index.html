WORLD BANK REQUIREMENTS — RESPONSE SUMMARY
1
Climate Integration
Through mobilization, capacity building, VDP/JDP
✓ Climate Ambassadors: 90 CAs (2/village, 1M+1F) — NEW role in AF POM. Trained on climate basics, GLOF risk assessment, adaptation options.
✓ Integration points: CA selection (Phase 1) → Climate risk mapping (Phase 2) → Climate-informed VDP/JDP (Phase 3) → Design screening (Phase 4) → Audit verification (Phase 5)
✓ Training: 3-day CA program covering climate science basics, participatory risk mapping, local adaptation strategies, GLOF early warning
2
Village Profile & Govt Alignment
Templates and government planning integration
✓ Village Profiles: 46 profiles covering demographics, infrastructure, services, climate vulnerabilities. Template aligned with government database format.
✓ Data flow: Village Profile → Jamoat Database → District Development Plans. JDP priorities shared with jamoat administration for alignment.
✓ Government engagement: District/jamoat officials participate in orientations (NSIFT-led) and JPC membership
3
Component 2 Livelihoods
Integration in orientation materials
✓ Integrated at every level: District (C2 overview) → Jamoat (eligibility & access) → Village (livelihood opportunities) → FGDs (entrepreneurs group) → JDP (infrastructure-livelihood linkages)
✓ Cycle 2 focus: Economic investment orientation — markets, processing, storage facilities
✓ Materials: C2 information included in all orientation packages and VPC/JPC training materials
4
Special Tracking Indicators
2 specific trackers required
✓ Tracker 1: VPC/JPC members who are or become local government employees → measures governance strengthening & institutional linkage
✓ Tracker 2: Community priorities funded by other sources (government, NGOs, private sector) → measures catalytic effect of CDD planning
✓ Mechanism: CDAs collect data monthly. TFP M&E Expert aggregates. Included in monthly & phase progress reports.
5
Progress Reporting
Procedures and frequency
✓ Monthly reports: Activities vs. plan, challenges, budget, participation data (gender/youth disaggregated), both special trackers, photos
✓ Phase reports: Submitted at end of each phase (0–6) with cumulative data
✓ Chain: CDAs (daily collection) → TFP (monthly consolidation to NSIFT) → NSIFT (forwards to WB)
